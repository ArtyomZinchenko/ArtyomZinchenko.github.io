Research Interests

My research interests include:

  • Cognitive neuroscience and neural mechanisms of cognition
  • Visual perception and attention
  • Perceptual decision making and confidence
  • Neuroimaging methods (fMRI, EEG, MEG)
  • Computational modeling of brain function
  • Metacognition and awareness
  • Statistical analysis and data science methods

About My Research

As a cognitive neuroscientist, I investigate how the brain processes information, makes perceptual decisions, and generates conscious awareness. My work combines behavioral experiments, neuroimaging techniques, and computational modeling to understand the neural basis of cognition. I am particularly interested in the mechanisms underlying visual perception, attention, and the confidence we have in our perceptual decisions.
