Research Interests

My research interests include:

  • Cognitive neuroscience
  • Neural mechanisms of perception and cognition
  • Neuroimaging methods (fMRI, EEG)
  • Computational modeling of brain function
  • Cognitive psychology
  • Experimental psychology
  • Statistical analysis and data science

About My Research

As a cognitive neuroscientist, I investigate the fundamental mechanisms of how the brain processes information, makes decisions, and generates conscious experience. My work combines behavioral experiments, neuroimaging techniques, and computational modeling to understand the neural basis of cognition.

Open Science Commitment: I am committed to transparent and reproducible research practices, including open data sharing and preregistration of studies.
