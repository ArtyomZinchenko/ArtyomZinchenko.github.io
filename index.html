        <div class="section">

            <h2>About My Research</h2>

            <p>

                As a cognitive neuroscientist, I investigate the fundamental mechanisms 

                of how the brain processes information, makes decisions, and generates 

                conscious experience. My work combines behavioral experiments, 

                neuroimaging techniques, and computational modeling to understand 

                the neural basis of cognition.

            </p>
